Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve businesses in the Lower Mainland area of British Columbia, including Burnaby, Vancouver, Richmond, Surrey, and surrounding areas.

Do you offer emergency IT support?

Yes, we offer emergency IT support for our clients. Our team is available to address critical issues outside of regular business hours for clients with support contracts.

How quickly can you respond to service requests?

Our standard response time is within 2-4 hours for regular service requests. For emergency situations, we aim to respond within 1 hour. Response times may vary based on your service level agreement.

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes, we offer free initial consultations to assess your IT needs and provide recommendations. Contact us to schedule your consultation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various payment methods including credit cards, direct bank transfers, and checks. We also offer online payment options through our secure payment portal.
