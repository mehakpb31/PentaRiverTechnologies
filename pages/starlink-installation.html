Frequently Asked Questions About Starlink Installation

How long does a Starlink installation take?

Most installations take 2-4 hours, depending on complexity. Roof installations may take longer than wall or pole mounts. We'll provide a time estimate during your site assessment.

Do you offer financing or payment plans?

Yes! We offer flexible payment options to make professional Starlink installation accessible. Contact us to discuss your options.

What if my property has obstructions like trees?

We specialize in challenging installations. Our site assessment identifies solutions, whether that's finding a clear location, trimming obstructions, or using pole mounting for better sky view.

Can you install Starlink on any roof type?

Yes! We install on metal, asphalt, tile, and other roof types. We use appropriate flashing and sealing to ensure weatherproofing and prevent leaks.

Do you service remote areas like Celista?

Absolutely! We specialize in remote Okanagan areas. Celista and other hard-to-reach properties are exactly where we excel. Contact us for service availability in your area.

What's included in your installation service?

Our service includes site assessment, professional mounting, cable routing, router setup, network configuration, and user training. We handle everything so you can enjoy your new internet immediately.
