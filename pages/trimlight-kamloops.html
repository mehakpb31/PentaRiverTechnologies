Trimlight Permanent Holiday Lighting in Kamloops, BC

From winter snowfall to hot, dry summers, Trimlight is built for the local climate and designed to elevate curb appeal year-round.

Why Kamloops homeowners choose permanent outdoor LED lighting

  • Holiday lighting, warm white architectural lighting, game day themes, Canada Day, Halloween, Diwali, and more.
  • Choose colors, brightness, schedules, scenes, and animations from your phone.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services.

Does Trimlight handle Kamloops winters and wind?

Yes. Trimlight is designed for Canadian conditions and is professionally installed with secure mounting and proper routing to protect components.

Can Trimlight be used after Christmas?

Absolutely. Many clients keep a warm white architectural look most nights, then switch to themes for holidays and events.

Is Trimlight visible during the day?

The system is installed under the soffit and fascia using color-matched channels for a low-profile daytime appearance.

Can you install on different soffit and fascia materials?

Yes. We evaluate your exterior materials and select the best mounting approach for a clean, durable finish.

Do you offer maintenance and repairs?

Yes. If you ever need service, we are local and can support your system.
