Why Kamloops homeowners choose permanent outdoor LED lighting

  • Year‑round use: holiday lighting, warm white architectural lighting, game day themes, Canada Day, Halloween, Diwali, and more.
  • App control: choose colors, brightness, schedules, scenes, and animations from your phone.
  • Low-profile look: channels are color-matched and mounted under soffits/fascia for a “disappears by day” finish.
  • Durability: designed for Canadian weather and professionally installed for long-term reliability.

FAQ: Trimlight Kamloops

Does Trimlight handle Kamloops winters and wind?

Yes—Trimlight is designed for Canadian conditions and is professionally installed with secure mounting and proper routing to protect components.

Can Trimlight be used after Christmas?

Absolutely. Many clients keep a warm white architectural look most nights, then switch to themes for holidays and events.

Is Trimlight visible during the day?

The system is installed under the soffit/fascia using color-matched channels for a low-profile daytime appearance.

Can you install on different soffit/fascia materials?

Yes. We evaluate your exterior materials and select the best mounting approach for a clean, durable finish.

Do you offer maintenance and repairs?

Yes. If you ever need service, we’re local and can support your system.
