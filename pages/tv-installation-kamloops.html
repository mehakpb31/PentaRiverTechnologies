Professional TV wall mounting for Kamloops homes

If you want a clean, modern setup, professional TV mounting is the difference between “good enough” and truly finished. We mount TVs on drywall, wood, and masonry (as applicable), confirm stud locations, and choose hardware that supports your TV size and viewing goals.

Fireplace TV mounting and articulated mounts

Fireplace mounting is common in Kamloops living rooms—especially in newer builds. We’ll help you choose the right mount (fixed, tilt, or full-motion) and set an ergonomic viewing angle. We can also advise on heat management and recommended clearances.

Wire concealment and cable management

Loose cables ruin the look. We offer multiple wire-hiding options depending on your wall type, layout, and budget:

  • In-wall concealment (where permitted and appropriate)
  • Paintable raceways for fast, tidy results
  • Structured cable routing to keep devices organized

Soundbar, home theatre, and smart device setup

We can mount soundbars, connect streaming devices, integrate gaming consoles, and ensure your system is configured correctly. If you want a cleaner install, we’ll route cables for a professional, showroom-style finish.

Commercial TV installation & digital signage in Kamloops

For businesses, we install commercial displays and digital signage for restaurants, retail shops, offices, gyms, and event spaces across Downtown Kamloops, along the Trans-Canada corridor, and near TRU. We can handle multi-screen layouts, clean cabling, and reliable mounting solutions designed for public spaces.

Areas we serve

We serve Kamloops and nearby communities across the Thompson-Nicola region, including Aberdeen, Sahali, Juniper Ridge, Brocklehurst, Westsyde, Valleyview, Dallas, and the North Shore.

How our TV installation process works

  1. Consultation: TV size, wall type, mount preference, device locations.
  2. Placement: confirm height and viewing angle; locate studs/structure.
  3. Install: mount, level, secure, and test connections.
  4. Finish: cable concealment and tidy device setup.

TV installation pricing in Kamloops

Pricing depends on TV size, mount type, wall material, wire concealment needs, and add-ons like soundbars or multi-display setups. We provide clear estimates before work begins.
